🌍 Daily Felix – City of the Day

A digital travel companion delivering AI-curated inspiration from one city every day.

⸻

🔑 Core Idea

Each day, users discover a new city with highlights, culture, and travel insights — turning daily micro-learning into wanderlust and trip-planning fuel.

⸻

📌 Key Features

1. Daily City Content (AI-powered)
	•	Featured city of the day (rotates across U.S. and world).
	•	Highlights: history, top 3 attractions, local food to try, best budget tip.
	•	Bonus: “Did you know?” quirky fact or cultural insight.

2. Customer Dashboard (interactive cards)
	•	Morning: “Wake up in…” city intro + main landmark.
	•	Afternoon: Food + cultural tip.
	•	Evening: Budget tip + local tradition.
	•	Travel inspiration cards are swipeable and quick to consume.

3. Admin Panel
	•	You (admin) can pick a city manually or let AI auto-generate.
	•	AI generates summaries (history, attractions, food, tips).
	•	Option to “pin” special cities (tie-in promotions, affiliates, seasonal).

4. Smart Notifications
	•	Up to 3 reminders daily (not as heavy as 18):
	•	Morning → Today’s City
	•	Midday → Fun Fact or Food
	•	Evening → Travel Tip

5. PWA Ready
	•	Works offline → users can “collect” daily cities like digital postcards.
	•	Installable app on mobile & desktop.

6. User Authentication
	•	Free tier: basic daily city card.
	•	Premium tier ($5–7/month): unlocks deep dive content (detailed itineraries, affiliate links, deals for that city).

⸻

🎯 Use Cases
	•	Travel Enthusiasts: Daily wanderlust hit.
	•	Families: Educational tool to teach kids about world cities.
	•	Travel Planners: Build a “bucket list” over time.
	•	Affiliate Tie-in: Each city card can have “Book Hotels/Experiences in [City]” link → monetization.

⸻

💡 Example Day Flow (City: Lisbon)
	•	Morning Card: “Good morning! Today’s city is Lisbon 🇵🇹. Explore the iconic Belém Tower, a UNESCO site by the Tagus River.”
	•	Afternoon Card: “Try Lisbon’s sweet treat — Pastel de Nata. Locals eat it with coffee in the afternoon.”
	•	Evening Card: “Budget tip: Buy a 24-hour tram pass for $7 instead of single tickets.”
	•	Fun Fact: “Did you know? Lisbon is older than Rome and one of the oldest cities in Western Europe.”
	•	Affiliate Button: “👉 Book a Lisbon walking tour with GetYourGuide.”

⸻

💰 Monetization
	•	Free tier: 1 city/day.
	•	Premium tier: unlock deep itineraries, maps, flight deal alerts.
	•	Affiliate links: tours, hotels, flights tied to daily city.
	•	Ads: tasteful, optional (like featured partner cities).

⸻

🔑 Tagline Ideas
	•	City of the Day — Explore the World, One Day at a Time.
	•	Daily Felix: City of the Day — Small Steps to Big Journeys.
	•	City of the Day — Your Daily Passport.

👉 So this becomes a self-paced, light-touch product:
